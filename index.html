ABOUT US

The Black.Codes is a non-profit organization that provides
a tech driven environment for the black community.
We use our tech based skills to provide growth and development
in the hopes that it will decrease the economic and
social disadvantages within the community. The Black.Codes is based in Nashville, TN but plans to grow nationally.
